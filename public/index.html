The Seven Truths

coRE

Cooperative Research and Development

Expanding the frontiers of artificial intelligence and the human mind. We believe in a future where human ingenuity is amplified by computational leverage.

AI can perform ALL cognitive tasks better than humans when properly specified. Humanity's role has transitioned from task execution to task understanding. The ability to communicate your intent to AI is the new human agency.

A single human now has access to unprecedented leverage and the power to manipulate the leaver however they see fit. Human leverage lies now hinges on execution, not learning.

Human bandwidth is the sole remaining scarce resource in an age of infinite computational leverage. Leverage is infinite, but the capacity to direct that leverage is finite, bound by the biological limits of a single human's physical actions.

Artificial Intelligence access is commoditized, however the dynamic of the interaction will never be commoditized. One's ability to extract value from AI is still in its infancy, but is evolving at an exponential rate. One's ability to ask better questions, recognize quality answers, know how to chain interactions, and having proper taste to curate outputs is the new frontier.

The new literacy is specifying, not knowing. Problem decomposition and specification as fundamental skill. Ability to precisely guide the desired outcomes and recognize when they've been achieved.

Ability to recognize excellence becomes primary creative contribution while curation becomes a dominant form of creativity.

Absolute knowledge is the only way to achieve alignment.
